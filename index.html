Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the tournamen

How can I buy World Cup tickets?

Tickets can be purchased through the official FIFA ticketing platform. Visit FIFA.com/tickets for more information.

When does the tournament begin?

The FIFA World Cup 2026 begins on June 11, 2026 with the opening match at Estadio Azteca, Mexico City.

Which cities are hosting the matches?

Matches will be played across 16 host cities in Canada, Mexico, and the USA including New York, Mexico City, Toronto, Vancouver, Los Angeles, Miami, and more.

How many teams are participating?

A total of 48 teams will participate in the FIFA World Cup 2026, expanded from the previous 32-team format.

Where can I watch the matches?

Matches will be broadcasted worldwide through official FIFA broadcast partners. Check your local listings for details.
